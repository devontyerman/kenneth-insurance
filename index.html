Why Final Expense Insurance

Protect your family from
unexpected costs.

The average funeral costs over $7,000. Final expense insurance makes sure your family never has to worry about covering those costs.

Affordable Premiums

Coverage typically costs $30–$70/month. Premiums are locked in and never increase, no matter your age.

No Medical Exam

Most plans require just a few health questions. No needles, no doctor visits, no waiting for lab results.

Protect Your Family

Benefits are paid directly to your beneficiary, tax-free. They can use the funds for funeral costs, bills, or anything they need.
