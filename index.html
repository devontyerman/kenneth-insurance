Coverage Options

The 3 types of life insurance,
explained simply.

Not sure which is right for you? That's exactly what the consultation is for — here's the quick version.

Term Life
Pure Protection.

Simple, affordable coverage for a set period of time.

  • Lowest monthly premium of the three options
  • Coverage for 10, 20, or 30 years
  • Tax-free payout to your family if you pass
  • Best for: income replacement, mortgage protection
Learn If Term Is Right For Me
Most Flexible
Growth + Protection.

IUL — Indexed Universal Life

  • Cash value grows linked to a market index
  • Protected from downturns — floor of 0% loss
  • Lifetime coverage with flexible premiums
  • Best for: building tax-advantaged wealth
Learn If IUL Is Right For Me
Whole Life
Lifelong Value.

Permanent coverage that builds guaranteed cash value.

  • Coverage that never expires while premiums are paid
  • Guaranteed cash value growth over time
  • Fixed premiums — they never increase
  • Best for: estate planning, final expenses
